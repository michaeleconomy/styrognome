Office Workers

Multiplayer officecore platformer - Abandoned

In this crazy office platformer for 2-4 players, employees complete tasks like making copies and answering phones within a time limit to avoid termination. Increasingly complex objectives make for very entertaining gameplay. Office Workers is inspired by Overcooked, Office Space, The Office, Castlevania, and 90s office culture. Targeting a release on XBox, PC, Mac, and Linux.
